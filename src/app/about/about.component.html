About Us

RURI is a global brand of natural gemstones and Jewelry. We promote to build sustainable supply chain of precious gems in the world's leading gem-producing countries, primarily Sri Lanka. RURI was launched in Japan in 2021 and we are headquartered in Ginza, Tokyo.
